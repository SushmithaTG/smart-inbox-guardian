# Vision

## Why am I building this?

One day I missed a company's confirmation email because it went into my spam folder.

That made me realize many students miss interviews, coding tests, and offer letters because important emails get lost.

So I decided to build Smart Inbox Guardian.

## Goal

Smart Inbox Guardian will help job seekers by:

- Detecting important career emails
- Sending instant alerts
- Summarizing long emails using AI
- Helping users never miss career opportunities
